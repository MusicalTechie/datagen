Create a table in Demand Planning
==========
In Oracle Fusion SCM → Supply Chain Planning → Demand Planning, you do not create a physical database “table” from scratch. Demand Planning works off:
	- Pre-seeded fact tables (planning measures)
	- Dimension hierarchies (Item, Organization, Customer, Time, etc.)
	- Measures inside a Planning Measure Catalog

What you are really creating is either:
	- A new measure (to hold bookings history or a custom calculation), or
	- A new table layout (view) in a worksheet to display measures across a time range.

Below is the correct minimal path for your specific objective:
	- Show Bookings History (24 months past) + Forecast (12 months future)
